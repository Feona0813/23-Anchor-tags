Serena Jameka William, born September 26, 1981, is an American professional tennis player. Williams was born in Saginaw, Michigan and is the youngest of five daughters including her sister, Venus Williams. When the children were young, the family moved to Compton,California , where Williams started playing tennis at the age of four. In addition to being a tennis superstar, Serena is a model, activist, entertainer, philanthropist and author.
